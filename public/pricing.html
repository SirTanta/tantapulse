Common questions

What's included in a sample?

You'll receive up to 50 scored leads in your chosen niche and city, delivered as a clean export. It's the same data format as the paid tiers — no watermarked preview, no stripped fields.

How does the weekly delivery work?

Once you're on a paid plan, your feed lands in your inbox every Monday morning. The niche and geography are locked in at signup — just hand the list to your sales team.

Can I change plans later?

Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated. Downgrades apply at the next billing cycle.

What does "scored" mean?

Each lead is ranked by geographic density, category relevance, and online presence signals. Your top contacts are flagged for first outreach.

Is there a contract?

No. Monthly plans are month-to-month. You can cancel from your account portal anytime — no calls, no hassle.

What geography is available?

Currently scoped to U.S. cities. Starter covers one metro per subscription. Pro and Agency cover up to three metros or niche combinations.

No refunds on monthly plans

Because leads are delivered instantly upon subscription activation, we don't offer refunds on monthly plans. Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period. Start with a free sample first — it's the best way to evaluate the data before committing.
